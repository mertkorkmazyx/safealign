Laser Shaft Alignment

Laser Shaft Alignment Services

Accurate shaft alignment is vital for the safety, efficiency, and long-term performance of large transport vessels. Even the smallest misalignment can lead to bearing wear, excessive vibration, energy loss, and costly repairs.

With advanced laser shaft alignment technology, we perform hot alignment and cold alignment checks with micron-level precision to ensure the reliability of your marine engines.

Benefits
  • Micron-level precision with laser systems
  • Faster alignment and adjustment compared to traditional methods
  • Reliable and repeatable results meeting international standards

Hot And Cold Alignment

Thermal compensation alignment services for various operating conditions and temperatures.

Proper shaft alignment is essential for the safe and efficient operation of main engines. Misalignment can cause bearing wear, increased vibration, energy loss, and even serious failures. To prevent these risks, Cold Alignment and Hot Alignment measurements are carried out.

Cold Alignment
  • Performed when the engine is cold and not in operation
  • Ensures that the shaft line is correctly aligned after a new installation, major maintenance, or overhaul
  • Provides a secure baseline setup before the engine starts running
Hot Alignment
  • Performed when the engine reaches its normal operating temperature
  • Considers thermal expansion and bearing clearances to analyze the system under real operating conditions
  • Verifies whether the cold alignment remains safe and efficient during operation

Jacking Test Services

The Jacking Test is a critical procedure for large marine engines and shaft lines, used to verify proper load distribution and alignment of the propeller shaft and bearings. It helps detect any overloading, deflection, or misalignment along the shaft line.

What is a Jacking Test?
  • The shaft line is lifted at specific points using hydraulic jacks to measure load distribution
  • The measured values are compared with manufacturer tolerances and international standards
  • Any abnormal bearing loads or misalignment issues can be detected

Propeller and Rudder Installation

The installation of propellers and rudders is one of the most critical stages in shipbuilding and maintenance. Improper installation can lead to increased vibration, fuel inefficiency, poor maneuverability, and reduced equipment lifetime.

Propeller Installation
  • Propellers are mounted to the shaft line with micron-level precision
  • Laser shaft alignment is applied to verify shaft line accuracy
  • Cone surfaces and keying elements are fitted in compliance with manufacturer standards
  • Post-installation balance and vibration tests ensure reliable performance
Rudder Installation
  • Rudder stock and bearings are aligned and fitted with strict tolerances
  • Load distribution and clearance are checked to prevent excessive wear
  • Hydraulic systems and steering gear are tested to ensure safe maneuvering

Sterntube Laser Adjustment

Sterntube laser adjustment is a critical procedure to ensure the safe, efficient, and long-lasting operation of marine engines and shaft lines. Misalignment of sterntube bearings can result in excessive friction, oil leakages, bearing wear, shaft damage, and reduced fuel efficiency.

What is Sterntube Laser Adjustment?
  • Using advanced laser alignment systems, the shaft line is checked against sterntube bearings to verify proper alignment
  • After installation or maintenance, the shaft is aligned with the bearings to ensure smooth operation
  • If deviations are detected, adjustments are made to prevent premature wear and extend bearing life
